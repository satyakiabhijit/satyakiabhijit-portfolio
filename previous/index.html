About Me

Passionate Full-Stack Developer & ML Enthusiast crafting innovative digital solutions

I’m Abhijit Satyaki, a passionate Full-Stack Web Developer, Machine Learning enthusiast, and IT undergraduate at Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College.
